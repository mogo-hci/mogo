OVERVIEW

The Problem

Leisure travel is an indispensable routine for many people nowadays that helps them escape from a busy schedule and reward themselves after working hard. It is expected to refresh and revitalize the traveler’s’ body and soul. However, almost every individual experiences some level of travel stress during their planned trip, such as failing to deal with unexpected situations.

The Solution

We are designing a smartwatch and smartphone paired application, MOGO, that takes an individual’s travel itinerary as its input, learns their personal preferences along the way to provide helpful travel tips and re-plans individual’s trip whenever needed.

Features

Personalized Recommendation

Tracks and learns your preference and provides personalized recommendations.

Real-time Support

Instant notification on your watch and get you the most up-to-date information.

Quick Replan

Powerful calculations to optimize your travel choices and improve your travel experience.

Travel Intelligence

Does the work and takes care of all the adjustments and let’s you enjoy your trip.
